Untitled Universe

This universe is untitled obviously, and I kinda like that . This is where most of my OCs live,, it pretty much takes place on present-day Earth except there are anthro animals cuz yeah !! A major difference with this universe is that LGBTQ folk are widely accepted, and have been for quite some time ,, this means that gender-affirming care is easier to access and is much more advanced than the real world . Some extinct species and fictional species also exist in this universe (e.g. dragons, protogens, synths) !

Lynnae Ridge

This is an old universe I started when I was just getting into the furry subculture ,, some of the first furries I drew belong to this universe .
The idea of the universe has shifted over time, and it's not something I'm focusing on currently. Originally it was named "/'tʃɹæʃ/" (pronounced "trash"), and it was gonna be like a serious cartoon or something about characters whose species are ones considered to be pests, or "trash animals", living in the big city.

Currently I've envisioned the universe as being for a game of some sort, with the same characters and a serious story, set in a Canadian city named "Lynnae Ridge". It would be about a group of 5 teens investigating the disappearance of a friend, who was presumed to be dead after falling down the city's waterfall . They'd unravel clues that point towards a scientific organization residing in the city, which does perfectly normal research on the outside, but is harboring a dark secret with police & government involvement ,,
I'd want the game to be an Ape Escape-like platformer, with different gadgets to use ,, of course, I don't know how Ape Escape gameplay would tie into the story at all tho lol . I also have absolutely zero game dev experience, and I don't really wanna tackle this kind of thing any time soon cuz of that, which is fine lol . It's just a dream game idea that I've had for a while :)
