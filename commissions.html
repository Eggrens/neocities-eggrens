Fullbody w/ Background - $30 USD

This is the main stuff I do. Single character drawn with soft (digital) pencil and flat colouring (maybe some colour blending, but no shading/lighting). Backgrounds are usually digital painted, but I can do photos or other abstract backgrounds if u wish :>

Some terms & stuff:

  • I draw anthro furry characters,, I could maybe do feral or Pokémon, but they are not my expertise!
  • I use PayPal for payment. Give me an email and I'll send the invoice to you. Prices are in USD just cuz I think it'd be easier that way rather than converting to CAD from another currency lol
  • There's some stuff that I'm uncomfortable drawing. I'll tell you if I'm not willing to draw something, so if ur unsure please ask,, I'll provide some stuff that I won't draw outright:
    • Extreme violence (e.g. gore)
    • ABDL
    • Right-wing shit
  • No refunds. Unless like some absolute worst scenario happens where I'm not able to draw your commission, or it's been like several months and I haven't drawn it yet,,
  • Idk just be nice and have patience :)

The process:

  • If u have a specific idea for the drawing and/or a background in mind, providing details and references etc. helps a lot! Or you can just let me be as creative as I want with it, up to you :)
  • I'll send progress as I work on the art. You can ask for changes if you'd like! I'll send a rough sketch first, then I'll send another update once the drawing is nearly finished (i.e. sketch refined, coloured, but no background). Just don't ask for huge changes when it's nearly finished!
  • Once the drawing's done I'll send u a Google Drive link to download the .png file of it. I'll post the drawing on my socials, but u can ask for it not to be posted if u wish.
