Small update to the gallery,, Images are now .png instead of .webp, except for animated ones which are still .webp cuz it compresses nicer. I also wanted to change to png just cuz that's a nicer format for most people! And it means I don't have to do any conversion myself, cuz all my art gets saved as png.
